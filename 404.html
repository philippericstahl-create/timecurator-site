ABOUT

TIME CURATOR®

Your Collector’s Office

Philipp Stahl — Founder

Collecting watches is more than just owning something.

It’s about having a passion, being dedicated, knowing your stuff, being patient and, most importantly, creating a legacy.

Every significant timepiece has a collector who has looked for it, studied it, waited for the right moment, negotiated and truly cared about its history. When you trust us to guide a watch to its next owner, it’s not just a deal; it’s an honour.

Time Curator® was made to take that responsibility seriously.

We see each watch as part of a collector’s personal story. We carefully analyse, curate and position every timepiece with the same passion and dedication with which it was collected. Our job is to protect its cultural value, make its story stronger and ensure it goes to the right people all over the world.

Every placement starts privately.
Discreetly. Personally. Thoughtfully positioned.
